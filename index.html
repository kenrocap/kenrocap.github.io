Team

Piyush was with Sequoia India (Peak XV), India/SEA’s largest VC firm, for the last 7 years, where he headed strategic development and generated an industry-leading exit execution track record with $2Bn+ cash exits. Prior to this, Piyush spent 17 years in technology investment banking at Morgan Stanley and Deutsche Bank, where he developed a deep understanding of tech companies while executing IPOs, M&A, and private placement. Across his career in the region, he has been instrumental in >30 IPOs and public market follow-ons. Piyush holds an undergraduate degree from SRCC, Delhi University, and a management degree from IIM Ahmedabad

Norbert is one of the most experienced secondary investment specialists in India. Leading TR Capital’s India investments from 2017 till recently, he has closed several noteworthy secondary transactions and is a founding member of the IVCA Secondaries Special Interest Group. Norbert started his investing career with Temasek and then went on to co-found IvyCap Ventures, creating a strong track record of returns. His work has won industry recognition, including from PEI and IAAIF. Norbert holds an undergraduate degree from IIT Kanpur and a management degree from IIM Calcutta

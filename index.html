    Our Story


    Piyush headed Strategic Development for India and SEA’s largest VC firm, Sequoia India (and now known as Peak XV) for the last 7 years, where he saw first-hand a growing need for liquidity options for a rising wave of maturing VC primary investments. With scarce M&A exits, a high bar for a company to conduct an IPO and a severe paucity of focused secondary funds, he stepped out of Peak XV to launch the growth stage and direct secondaries focused strategy under Kenro Capital.

    The market proposition

    Secondaries Opportunities

    The VC industry in India & SEA has grown from average annual investment value of $6B over 2008 to 2017, to $35B average annual investment value over 2018 to 2024, representing $260B in total investment value to date.

    However, most regional VC funds’ distributions to their Limited Partners have failed to keep pace with their portfolio value growth, driven by a combination of long business maturation, still-maturing capital markets, scarce M&A exits and a paucity of dedicated secondary funds.

    As the VC industry matures, there is a growing awareness and alignment between key stakeholders to prioritize the pace of capital distribution via several means, including direct secondary sales and portfolio secondary sale solutions.

    The India & SEA secondaries opportunity is estimated at >$100B growing >25% annually.

    (Source: PitchBook, Tracxn)

    In addition to VC funds, the other key shareholders in start ups include Founders and employees, public market investors, corporates, family offices and the like.

    The India and SEA Secondaries Opportunity is estimated at over $100 Billion and growing at over 25% annually.

    Our Investment Approach

    We invest primarily in late stage, venture backed companies in India and Southeast Asia typically 2-3 years to IPO, via secondary purchases from existing shareholders.

    Our typical target investment size $20-30M investment per company, with flexibility to do larger amounts through co-investments with our limited partners

    Our focus is to invest in venture backed companies with the following characteristics:

    • Evidence of business and financial maturity
    • Strong leadership team
    • Minimal dilution from subsequent private raises
    • Cap table in need of liquidity solutions
    • Up to 3 years to IPO
